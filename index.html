Blackshields Home Improvements is a Belper based business which prides itself on offering a reliable and high quality service in the Belper and Derby areas.

Established in 2007, we quickly built up a reputation for attention to detail in all our jobs, whether large or small.

Whilst initially focussing on exterior work, demand from clients meant the business rapidly developed into one which offers a comrehensive and professional range of services for home and garden.

Our Services

  • Landscaping and Gardening

    We offer a wide range of landscaping and gardening services, including:

    • garden design and planting advice
    • paving projects and patios
    • decking
    • fencing and trellis
    • garden clearance
    • raised beds and borders

  • Kitchens and Bathrooms

    We can provide the following refurbishment or installation services for your kitchen and bathroom:

    • stripping out of old kitchen or bathroom
    • floor and wall tiling
    • painting and decorating
    • kitchen and bathroom installation
    • qualified electrician

  • Painting and Decorating

    We provide a full range of painting and decorating services, such as:

    • internal and external work
    • minor repair of window frames and other woodwork
    • floor painting, staining or varnishing
    • wallpapering
    • tiling
    • laying laminate and wooden floors

  • General Maintenance

    We offer a wide range of general services for your home and garden, including:

    • minor interior jobs (shelving, picture hanging, etc...)
    • lock replacement
    • flat pack assembly
    • minor electrical work
    • dry stone walling
    • gutter clearance
